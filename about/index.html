OPTIMIZING WORKFLOW

The Blueprint of Our Processes

01:Free Consultation

We start with a free consultation to understand your investment goals and budget. This allows us to tailor our services to meet your needs and provide you with the best opportunities.

02:Property Sourcing and Evaluation

Our licensed agents identify and evaluate potential properties that align with your investment objectives. We conduct thorough inspections and provide detailed assessments to help you make informed decisions.

03:Full Renovation Estimate

Once you've selected a property, our experienced team provides a comprehensive renovation estimate that outlines the scope of work and associated costs. This gives you a clear picture of the investment required.

04:Financing Assistance and Deal Analysis

We help set you up with a lender to secure financing for your investment. Additionally, we analyze the deal based on the rehab amount to determine the potential final profit. This step helps ensure that you have a clear understanding of the financial implications while the property is under contract.

05:Project Management

Our project manager oversees the entire renovation process, from selecting qualified subcontractors to coordinating schedules and ensuring quality control. We keep you updated on progress and address any concerns along the way.

06:Utility Setup and Winter Security Package

We set up essential utilities, provide winter security by removing furnaces and tanks, and offer snow removal and bi-weekly mowing to maintain and protect your properties year-round.

07:Post-Renovation Support

Once the renovation is complete, we offer post-rehab cleaning services and final inspections to ensure your property is ready for sale or rent. Our team is here to assist you every step of the way.

08:Sale or Rental Setup

When the property is ready, we will help you either sell it for a profit or set it up for renting. If you choose to rent the property, we will connect you with one of our partnered rental management companies to ensure a smooth transition and efficient property management.
